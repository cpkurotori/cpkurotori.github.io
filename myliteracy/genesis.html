Genesis

In the beginning... there were computers.

And it was good.

I

was born in 1995, about fifteen years after the start of the Digital Revolution[1]. Although I have seem many changes and improvements to technology, I will never know of a time without computers. With that being said, computers and technology have been an integral part of my life since I was a child. Throughout my life, there is one story that my dad keep's bringing up. When I was 3 years old, we had a family computer in the living room that my mom, my dad, and my sister used. On this large desktop, running Windows 95, my dad had downloaded a program called Napster. This program/service had a database of music that was able to be downloaded for free to any personal computer. Being the child that I was, loving the Disney movies that we had on VHS tapes, I really wanted to download the soundtrack of The Lion King. Just a reminder... (Hover over me!)
